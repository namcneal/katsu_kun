Welcome to 活かつくん, the Japanese conjugation game!

With ru-verbs, u-verbs, causative-passives, and a myriad of forms rolling around in your head, it can be hard to keep track of all of them. Here, you can develop the necessary experience with verb conjugation and improve your skills!

To start, simply use the buttons below to choose the types of verbs and conjugation forms you want to practice. Your selected options will turn yellow. Once that's done, press "Start", and the game will begin!
